Our Services

Pioneer Hospitalities offers a comprehensive suite of services, specializing in industrial, commercial, and institutional catering, along with managing guest houses, providing housekeeping for various facilities, and overseeing hostels for schools and colleges.

INDUSTRIAL CANTEEN
Pioneer Hospitalities caters to a prestigious clientele in the industrial sector, including top brands like Hindalco, LNG Petronet, L&T, Meghmani Organics, and Benzo Chem. Our catering services adhere to stringent quality standards, ensuring hygienic food preparation overseen by skilled chefs and quality controllers. We serve leading corporate houses, industrial workforces, and educational institutions with efficiency and excellence.
OUTDOOR SERVICES
Our commercial catering services are tailored for hotels, convention centers, and large corporations, specializing in business events, conventions, and meetings. We streamline operations to save time and energy for our clients, despite the potential cost implications due to demand charges. Our commitment to quality and service reliability remains unmatched.
PANTRY SERVICES
Pioneer Hospitalities provides centralized canteen management solutions essential for organizations with large workforces. Our efficient system ensures seamless food service delivery, catering to employees who depend on canteen facilities for their daily meals. We prioritize efficiency and effectiveness in meeting organizational dining needs
GUEST HOUSE
PHS offers bespoke guest house management services, ensuring round-the-clock care with professionally trained in-house cooks and housekeeping staff. Our services mirror those of renowned hotel chains, meeting the exacting standards demanded by corporate clients for cost-effectiveness and punctual service delivery.
HOUSE KEEPING
As a leading provider in Central Gujarat, Pioneer Hospitalities excels in housekeeping services for offices, homes, industries, and hospitals. Our services are celebrated for their reliability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness. We maintain industry-leading standards, ensuring every client receives meticulous attention to cleanliness and operational efficiency.
MAN POWER SUPPLY
Pioneer Hospitalities distinguishes itself with high-quality manpower supply across diverse industrial sectors. Our dedicated team of technicians and workers is adept at managing various operations efficiently, offering competitive pricing and timely service delivery. We prioritize client satisfaction by providing skilled personnel tailored to specific industry requirements.
