About PHS

Pioneer Hospitality Services proudly stands as a dynamic and fast-growing company, expertly managed with a team of skilled professionals. We excel in offering diverse corporate services in the realms of industrial, commercial, and institutional catering. Our unwavering commitment is to provide top-tier food and services, ensuring the complete satisfaction of our valued customers.

We provide meticulous management of corporate guest houses, superior housekeeping services, and create welcoming and comfortable environments for all guests. Our services extend to the efficient and hygienic management of school and college hostels, catering to the nutritional and housekeeping needs of students, and fostering a safe, healthy, and conducive living environment for learning.

Our advanced Central Kitchen, strategically located in Dahej Dist- Bharuch, Dahej, allows us to efficiently serve clients outside the company premises. The facility is fully equipped to maintain the utmost hygienic conditions and supreme cleanliness in all aspects of cooking, storage, and food preparation.

To ensure the quality and hygiene of our food during transit, we operate a dedicated fleet of vehicles equipped with packed and sealed containers, ensuring that our food arrives fresh and in perfect condition, ready to be enjoyed by our clients in and around Dahej.
