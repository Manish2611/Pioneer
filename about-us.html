About PHS

Pioneer Hospitality Services is proud to be one of the fastest-growing, professionally managed companies with a highly skilled and dedicated workforce. We offer a comprehensive range of corporate services, specializing in industrial, commercial, and institutional catering. Our mission is to deliver top-quality food and services, ensuring the complete satisfaction of our customers.We provide expert management of guest houses and housekeeping services for companies, as well as overseeing hostels for schools and colleges. Our commitment to excellence ensures that every service we offer meets the highest standards of quality and professionalism.

Our Central Kitchen, strategically located in Dahej Dist- Bharuch, is fully equipped to maintain the utmost hygienic conditions and supreme cleanliness in cooking, storage, and food preparation. This state-of-the-art facility allows us to efficiently cater to clients beyond the company premises.

To guarantee the freshness and hygiene of our food during transit, we have a fleet of vehicles equipped with packed and sealed containers. This ensures that our prepared meals arrive at various locations in and around Dahej in perfect condition.

Additionally, our centralized administrative office is managed by experienced professionals and equipped with the latest communication facilities, including internet, fax, and telephones. This ensures smooth and efficient operations, allowing us to serve our clients effectively.

Pioneer Hospitality Services has quickly earned a reputation for excellence due to our exquisite and innovative recipes, spectacular presentation, and unfailing service. Our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction has enabled us to build strong, lasting relationships with our valued customers.
